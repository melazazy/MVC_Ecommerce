Admin Dashboard

  • User Management

    • Create, edit, or delete user accounts.
    • Manage user roles and permissions.
    • View user activity and login history.

  • Product Management

    • Add new products to the catalog.
    • Edit product details, including name, description, price, and images.
    • Organize products into categories.
    • Manage product inventory and stock levels.
    • Set product attributes like size, color, and brand.

  • Order Management

    • View and process customer orders.
    • Update order statuses (e.g., pending, shipped, delivered).
    • Generate packing slips and invoices.
    • Handle returns, refunds, and exchanges.
    • Monitor order fulfillment and shipping.

  • Inventory Management

    • Track product stock levels and receive notifications for low inventory.
    • Manage product variants (e.g., sizes, colors) and their availability.
